About Me

Hello there, I'm Sam! I'm a fullstack developer. My web stack:

  • HTML5 + CSS3
  • ReactJS
  • NodeJS
  • PostgreSQL
  • Heroku
  • OpenAPI 3

I spend most of my time learning new frameworks and languages and working on projects with Node and React, but outside of those times, I am usually playing videogames, reading books, or out hanging on to the side of a wall trying not to fall and break a leg or two (Yay for rock climbing!). Whether I'm working solo or in a team, I'm always open to improve my code and myself!


I began learning about programming due to my curiosity about videogames and computers during my last year of high school, and I quickly knew that I wanted to become a developer. I was (and still am) fascinated with the process of starting with a blank slate, and shaping it into what you wish.
